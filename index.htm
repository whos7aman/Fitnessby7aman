OUR STORY

Gymshark was founded by school friends Ben Francis and Lewis Morgan in 2012. Both aged 20, they set up the brand while studying at university. Initially, Gymshark started drop shipping bodybuilding supplements through its website.

In 2013, it began designing and manufacturing its own fitness apparel.
